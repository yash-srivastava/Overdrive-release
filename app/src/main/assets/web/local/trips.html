Driving DNA Breakdown
Tap rows for coaching 💡
🔮 Anticipation
--
Measures how well you read traffic ahead and lift off the accelerator early before braking. Higher scores mean less energy wasted on sudden stops.
💡 Tip: Coast more before red lights and slow traffic. Lift off the pedal 3–5 seconds earlier than usual.
🌊 Smoothness
--
Tracks how gradually you apply and release the accelerator and brake pedals. Jerky inputs waste energy and reduce comfort.
💡 Tip: Pretend there's a glass of water on the dashboard. Smooth pedal transitions keep it from spilling.
🎯 Speed Discipline
--
Evaluates how consistently you maintain appropriate speeds. Frequent speeding and large speed variations reduce this score.
💡 Tip: Use cruise control on highways. Keeping within 5 km/h of the limit saves significant energy.
⚡ Efficiency
--
Compares your energy consumption per km against the optimal baseline for your vehicle, route, and conditions.
💡 Tip: Pre-condition the cabin while plugged in. Avoid full-throttle launches from stops.
📐 Consistency
--
Measures how uniform your driving style is throughout the trip. Erratic behavior (alternating aggressive and calm) lowers this score.
💡 Tip: Pick a driving style and stick with it. Consistent habits build muscle memory for efficiency.
